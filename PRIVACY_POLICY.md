# Privacy Policy for SpiritAmmo

**Last updated: January 7, 2025**

## Introduction

SpiritAmmo ("we", "our", or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Audio Recordings
We request permission to access your microphone (`RECORD_AUDIO`) to allow you to:
- Record and practice verses spoken aloud
- Improve your memorization through audio playback
- Store recordings locally on your device for your practice sessions

**Important**: Your audio recordings are stored locally on your device only. We do NOT transmit, store, or have access to your recordings on our servers.

### Data Storage
- All practice data and recordings are stored locally on your device
- We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers
- Your data remains on your device and is never shared with third parties

## Permissions We Request

### Microphone Permission (RECORD_AUDIO)
- **Purpose**: To record your voice as you practice verses
- **Usage**: Audio is recorded, stored, and played back locally for your practice
- **Data handling**: Never uploaded or transmitted; stays on your device

### Camera Permission (if applicable)
- **Purpose**: To capture images for verse backgrounds or profile setup
- **Usage**: Images are stored locally on your device
- **Data handling**: Never uploaded or transmitted; stays on your device

## Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect your information:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data transmission to external servers
- You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time by clearing the app data

## Third-Party Services

SpiritAmmo does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your practice data remains completely private.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA and GDPR-K.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- **Access**: All your data is available within the app
- **Deletion**: Clear app data or uninstall the app to remove all stored information
- **Export**: Your recordings and practice data can be accessed through the app

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
